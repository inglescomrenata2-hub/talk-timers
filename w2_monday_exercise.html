Past Simple
Affirmative · Negative · Questions

Goal: Contar o que aconteceu no passado com confiança — verbos regulares e irregulares, negativas com didn't e perguntas com did.

regular -ed irregular didn't Did…?
Quick reference — como formar o Past Simple
AFFIRMATIVE
verb + ed / irregular
I walked · She went
NEGATIVE
didn't + base verb
I didn't go (NOT didn't went)
QUESTION
Did + subject + base
Did you go? (NOT Did you went?)
Fill in the Blank
01 Regular · Affirmative Complete no Past Simple.

Last night, we a great movie. (watch)

💡 Verbo regular → termina em -ed.

02 Irregular · Affirmative Complete no Past Simple.

She a new car last month. (buy)

💡 Verbo irregular — não é "buyed".

03 Negative Complete a negativa.

I you because I was busy. (not / call)

💡 didn't + verbo na base (sem -ed!). Aceita "didn't call".

04 Question Complete a pergunta (2 lacunas).

they on time? (arrive)

💡 Did + sujeito + verbo na base.

05 Irregular · Negative Complete a negativa.

They enough money. (not / have)

💡 Cuidado: depois de didn't o verbo volta pra base — have, não had. Aceita "didn't have".
